360° Degrees

360° Degrees is a dynamic pizza house poised to redefine the culinary landscape in the vibrant community of Leyte, 360° Degrees is committed to delivering an unparalleled dining experience centered around artisanal pizzas, exceptional service, and a welcoming atmosphere.

All Cheese

All cheese pizza is a timeless classic that celebrates the rich and indulgent flavors of cheese in every bite. Each slice offers a perfect balance of gooey, melted cheese atop a crispy crust, creating a symphony of textures and flavors that is sure to please cheese lovers of all ages.

Ham and Cheese

Ham and cheese, a timeless culinary pairing, come together harmoniously in this classic combination. Thinly sliced ham, typically cooked and seasoned to perfection, meets creamy, melty cheese atop a variety of bread or pastry. With each bite, the savory saltiness of the ham melds seamlessly with the creamy richness of the cheese, creating a satisfying and timeless taste sensation that is sure to delight palates of all ages.

Hawaiian

Hawaiian pizza is a delightful fusion of savory and sweet flavors, combining traditional pizza ingredients with a tropical twist. This iconic pie features a classic pizza base topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, savory ham, and juicy chunks of pineapple. Whether enjoyed as a quick bite or shared with friends and family, Hawaiian pizza offers a deliciously satisfying culinary experience that transports diners to a tropical paradise with every savory bite.

Bacon and Mushroom

The bacon and mushroom pizza is a mouthwatering blend of savory and earthy flavors that tantalize the taste buds with each bite. This classic pizza features a crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, savory bacon strips, and tender slices of earthy mushrooms. The smoky, salty notes of the bacon complement the mushrooms, creating a satisfyingly savory combination that is sure to please even the most discerning palate.
